About Us

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Discover the story behind COZYCUP Coffee House.

The COZYCUP Coffee House is the only coffee shop that will make you feel at home. It all started on August 27 in year 2020. We can call the owner as "coffeeholic", who is addicted and at the same time have love and passion for coffee. He started to create a small business and its not just a business but its his way of sharing the joy and happiness of drinking coffee. He started the serve simple hot and cold coffee but with a strong flavor and aroma as you take it.

As the time pass by, it become famous because whenever you drink its coffee, you will feel "cozy" which simply tells that "a feeling of comfort, warmth and relaxation" which gave the coffee house a name called "COZYCUP Coffee House".
